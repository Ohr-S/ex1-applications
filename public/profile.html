About me

Hi, my name is Ohr, I am a student of computer science in the second year at the IDC in Herzliya. Throughout my life I have volunteered in various organizations that help women who have been harmed. I went through many challenges in my life, which helped me formulate my dream. My dream is to help underprivileged populations, and do everything I can to change the welfare system in Israel. I believe that reality must change fundamentally so that women, and children at risk can have full equality of opportunity. I am looking for people to be by my side on this journey on the path to fulfilling the dream. People who think that the reality today does not allow underprivileged populations to reach their full potential. During my youth I worked in temporary jobs in parallel with volunteering with elderly people, people with disabilities and assistance to women in an organization called "Do Not Stand Back". I later ran a classical ballet studio and today I am on my way to my next role - probably in the cyber field.
